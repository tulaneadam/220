Liz Yarock, LCSW

Welcome! Thank you for your interest in working with me. My name is Liz Yarock and I am a Licensed Clinical Social Worker dedicated to helping individuals improve their lives. I provide a quiet, safe and private space where you can explore and talk about what’s on your mind and in your heart and feel truly supported in the process. I specialize in providing individual and group therapy for children, adolescents, young adults, and families. 

Areas I specialize in include:

  • Anxiety

  • OCD and Panic Attacks

  • Depression

  • Adolescent Relationships

  • Grief

  • Children Coping with Divorce and Separation

  • Life Transitions

  • Increased Self-Understanding

  • Stress Management

One of the most crucial components to successful therapy is feeling a sense of trust, comfort, and respect when working with a therapist. To ensure you feel confident in working with me, I am happy to offer a complimentary 15-minute phone consultation to help you get a sense of who I am and my approach to therapy in order to determine whether you and I both think we might work well together. During this initial phone call, I will be able to learn more about you, your goals for therapy, tell you about my practice and answer any questions or concerns you may have prior to scheduling an appointment.

To set up a phone consultation, or to schedule your first appointment, you may contact me via phone or email. I will respond to your inquiries within 24 hours.

Please call me today for your complimentary phone consultation. I look forward to hearing from you.
